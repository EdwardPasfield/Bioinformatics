About the Tool


This is a tool that searches a protein sequence and reports back any matched motifs, including the sequence and position of the motif.


Amyloid proteins contain short sequences that, in the right conditions or upon mutation, form beta – sheets which aggregate to form rigid fibrils. These proteins and aggregates are associated with a wide range of diseases, most notable neurodegenerative diseases such as Alzheimer’s disease and Parkinson’s disease. There is some debate as to whether the cause of these aggregates are due to specific sequences and properties of amino acids or is a generic trait of all proteins. This is a prediction tools which has been developed to predict the basis of sequence and structural information, a web based tool which predicts by sequence.
